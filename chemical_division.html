Chemical Division

At ANSS Consultant & Services Corp., our Chemical Division is dedicated to delivering high-performance, sustainable chemical solutions tailored to meet the demanding needs of industrial, energy, and environmental sectors. In collaboration with strategic partners, including global leaders like PSI Global Solutions, we bring cutting-edge chemistry to optimize operations, reduce costs, and support regulatory compliance.

Our offerings are built around innovation, efficiency, and environmental responsibility, ensuring that every chemical solution we deliver integrates seamlessly with your existing systems and enhances your overall process performance.

1. Desludging & Oil Recovery Solutions

Our sludge liquefaction and oil recovery systems are engineered for the energy industry, with a focus on tank cleaning, sludge management, and hydrocarbon recovery. Using proprietary PSI chemical technologies, we break down heavy sludge layers to create pumpable slurries—reducing downtime, enhancing safety, and recovering valuable hydrocarbons.

  • • Sludge Liquefaction Chemicals for online and offline tank cleaning
  • • Oil Recovery Enhancers that maximize hydrocarbon yield from sludge
  • • Hydrocarbon Separation Aids to streamline oil-water-solid separation
  • • Sludge Dewatering Additives to reduce waste volumes and handling costs
  • • Environmentally safe and hydrocarbon-compatible formulations for refinery and upstream tank applications
  • • Compatibility with automated tank cleaning systems, including jet mixers and circulation skids

2. Water & Wastewater Treatment Solutions

We provide innovative chemical treatment programs for industrial and municipal wastewater systems— targeting the removal of contaminants, optimizing biological performance, and enhancing sludge dewatering.

  • • Customized Catalyst Beds for advanced oxidation and effluent treatment
  • • Electrochemical Oxidation & Enhanced DAF Systems
  • • Flocculants, Coagulants, and pH Conditioners tailored to system needs
  • • Filter Press & Screw Press Dewatering Additives
  • • Integration with biological and hybrid systems:
    • - MBR (Membrane Bioreactor)
    • - MBBR (Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor)
    • - ASP (Activated Sludge Process)
    • - Fenton and Advanced Oxidation Processes
  • • Chemical dosing optimization to improve BOD/COD removal and system reliability
