POWER DIVISION

At ANSS Consultant & Services Corp., we provide end-to-end Electrical, Instrumentation & Control System Engineering Services across multiple voltage levels and industrial domains. Our expert team delivers comprehensive solutions that support efficient, safe, and standards-compliant project execution for utility, oil & gas, energy, petrochemical, and infrastructure sectors.

Electrical Engineering Services
  • • Electrical Power Distribution Configuration for HV, MV & LV systems (GIS & AIS up to 400kV: 400kV, 220kV, 132kV, 66kV, 33kV, 11kV, 6.6kV, 3.3kV, 415V, etc.)
  • • ETAP Studies including Short Circuit, Load Flow, and Power System Analysis
  • • Preliminary & Detailed Design:
    • Field Layouts & Single Line Diagrams
    • Equipment Sizing & System Configuration
    • Power/Control Cable Sizing & Schedule
    • Substation Layouts & Sectional Drawings
    • Cable Trench and Cable Tray Routing
    • Earthing and Lightning Protection Layouts
    • Lighting and Small Power Layouts
    • Standard Installation Details & Practices
  • • Tender Documentation:
    • Preparation of Technical Specs, BOQs, and Cost Estimations
Instrumentation & Control Systems
  • • Instrumentation Engineering:

  • • Automation & Control Systems:

Advanced System Study Services

Using industry-leading software like ETAP 22, DigiSilent, PSCAD, PV SYST, and MiPower, we offer advanced simulation and analytical services, including:

  • • Grid Compliance & Load Flow Studies
  • • Short Circuit and Harmonic Analysis
  • • Motor Starting Analysis
  • • Protection Relay Coordination & Arc Flash Studies
  • • Transient Stability Studies
  • • Transformer and Cable Energizing/De-energizing Analysis
  • • Insulation Coordination Studies
  • • Grounding, Induced Voltage & Touch Potential Studies
  • • Lightning Protection System Studies
  • • Typical Protection Coordination Curve Submissions
