Renewable Energy Division

  2. Renewable Energy Division

As a forward-thinking partner in sustainability, ANSS offers complete services across the solar and wind energy value chain.

With a focus on innovation and performance, our Renewable Energy Division delivers turnkey solutions from initial feasibility studies and engineering design to the final commissioning of solar and wind infrastructure. We help clients assess site viability, energy yield potential, and grid compliance requirements to ensure technically sound and financially viable projects.

ANSS also provides comprehensive Operations & Maintenance (O&M) services for renewable energy assets, ensuring sustained output and minimal downtime. Whether it's grid evacuation feasibility, remote monitoring, or preventive maintenance, we empower asset owners with reliable tools and technical support to maximize their return on investment in clean energy.

How can we help?

  • Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS):

    Powering Resilience, Efficiency, and Clean EnergyFutures

    At ANSS Consultant & Services Corp., our Renewable Energy Division is committed to advancing sustainable and smart energy infrastructure through state-of-the-art Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS). We deliver complete, scalable, and reliable energy storage solutions tailored for utilities, commercial-industrial clients, microgrids, and off-grid applications across Canada, the USA, Africa, and the Middle East.

    In collaboration with our technology partner, AmpereHour Solar Technology Pvt. Ltd., we bring global field experience and technical innovation to every project. AmpereHour has supported the successful deployment of BESS systems across diverse geographies, further strengthening our capability to deliver dependable and future-proof solutions.

    What We Deliver:

    • • Fully integrated, containerized Li-ion BESS units (10’, 20’, 40’ configurations)
    • • Advanced Lithium-ion Battery Packs (LFP Chemistry)
    • • Power Conversion Systems (PCS)
    • • Energy Management System (EMS)
    • • Fire Safety, HVAC & Protection Systems
    • • Cloud-Based Monitoring, Control & Optimization

    Our BESS platforms support both grid-tied and islanded applications, making them ideal for renewable integration, peak load management, and energy resiliency.

    ANSS Capabilities:

    • • System Design & Sizing
    • • Techno-Commercial Feasibility Studies
    • • Power System Analysis (Load Flow, Short Circuit, Arc Flash, Harmonic Studies)
    • • Grid Code Compliance Support
    • • Control Architecture Development & SCADA Integration
    • • Commissioning, Training & Lifecycle Support

    Applications We Serve:

    • • Renewable Energy Integration (Solar + Storage, Wind + Storage)
    • • Microgrid Development & Diesel Displacement
    • • Peak Shaving & Demand Charge Reduction
    • • Grid Frequency Regulation & Ancillary Services
    • • Critical Backup Power for Industrial Facilities
